This ZKP / Web3 Hackathon, hosted by Berkeley RDI and in partnership with ZKP MOOC (~3500 enrolled worldwide), aims to bring together all students, researchers, and practitioners to build and showcase their innovative work in ZKP.


Code your way to the top - 100.000$ and a trip to Berkeley await!

  • Get nominated for the ZKP summer cluster at Simons Institute at UC Berkeley! - supported with 100.000$ in scholarships!
  • Propose a self-selected project to be awarded the grand prize of up to 10.000$
  • Fulfill designated tasks, as proposed in each of the Hackathon repostitories - win individually assigned prizes!
